GOT QUESTIONS?

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is the conference held?

The conference will be held at the Moscone Center in San Francisco, California. The venue is easily accessible via public transportation and offers state-of-the-art facilities.

Is there a virtual attendance option?

Yes! We offer a virtual pass for $199 that includes live streaming of all keynotes and access to recordings for one year. However, workshops and networking events are in-person only.

What's the refund policy?

Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the event. After that, tickets can be transferred to another person but are non-refundable.

Are meals included?

All passes include breakfast and lunch each day. Pro and VIP passes also include the networking dinner on Day 2. Coffee and snacks are available throughout the day.

Can I get a student discount?

Absolutely! Students get 50% off any pass with valid student ID. Contact our support team with proof of enrollment to receive your discount code.
